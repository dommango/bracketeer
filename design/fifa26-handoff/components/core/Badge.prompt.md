Inline status pill. `live` pulses red, `gold` marks the leader/winner, `brand` uses pitch green.

```jsx
<Badge tone="live" variant="soft">Live · 73'</Badge>
<Badge tone="gold" variant="solid">Leader</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand" variant="outline">Group A</Badge>
<Badge tone="positive">+3 pts</Badge>
```

Variants: `solid` · `soft` · `outline`. Soft is the default — it's the chip you see most often in the leaderboard.
